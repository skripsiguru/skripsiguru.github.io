Mass Production involves making many copies of products, very quickly, using assembly line techniques to send partially complete products to workers who each work on an individual step, rather than having a worker work on a whole product from start to finish. Mass production of fluid matter typically involves pipes with centrifugal pumps or screw conveyors (augers) to transfer raw materials or partially complete product between vessels. Fluid flow processes such as oil refining and bulk materials such as wood chips and pulp are automated using a system of process control which uses various instruments to measure variables such as temperature, pressure, volumetric and level, providing feedback. Bulk materials such as coal, ores, grains and wood chips are handled by belt, chain, slat, pneumatic or screw conveyor bucket elevators and mobile equipment such as front-end loaders. Materials on pallets are handled with forklifts. Also used for handling heavy items like reels of paper, steel or machinery are electric overhead cranes, sometimes called bridge cranes because they span large factory bays.. Mass production is capital intensive and energy intensive, as it uses a high proportion of machinery and energy in relation to workers. It is also usually automated while total expenditure per unit of product is decreased. However, the machinery that is needed to set up a mass production line (such as robots and machine presses) is so expensive that there must be some assurance that the product is to be successful to attain profits. Mass Production involves making many copies of products, very quickly, using assembly line techniques to send partially complete products to workers who each work on an individual step, rather than having a worker work on a whole product from start to finish. Mass production of fluid matter typically involves pipes with centrifugal pumps or screw conveyors (augers) to transfer raw materials or partially complete product between vessels. Fluid flow processes such as oil refining and bulk materials such as wood chips and pulp are automated using a system of process control which uses various instruments to measure variables such as temperature, pressure, volumetric and level, providing feedback. Bulk materials such as coal, ores, grains and wood chips are handled by belt, chain, slat, pneumatic or screw conveyor bucket elevators and mobile equipment such as front-end loaders. Materials on pallets are handled with forklifts. Also used for handling heavy items like reels of paper, steel or machinery are electric overhead cranes, sometimes called bridge cranes because they span large factory bays.. Mass production is capital intensive and energy intensive, as it uses a high proportion of machinery and energy in relation to workers. It is also usually automated while total expenditure per unit of product is decreased. However, the machinery that is needed to set up a mass production line (such as robots and machine presses) is so expensive that there must be some assurance that the product is to be successful to attain profits. 36. Suatu alat berupa chain conveyor mengangkut bahan dengan jarak rata rata 2 meterdengat berat 150 dengan kapasitas 5 ton/jam.maka kecepatan dalam memindahkan bahan tersebut adalah


Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Dalam soal ini, diketahui jarak rata-rata yang ditempuh oleh chain conveyor adalah 2 meter dan kapasitasnya adalah 5 ton/jam. Untuk mencari kecepatan conveyor dalam memindahkan bahan tersebut, kita perlu mengetahui berapa berat bahan yang diangkut dalam satu detik. Karena kapasitas conveyor adalah 5 ton/jam, maka kapasitas conveyor dalam satu detik adalah 5000 kg/3600 s = 1.39 kg/s.

Dalam menghitung kecepatan conveyor, kita juga perlu mengetahui waktu yang dibutuhkan untuk memindahkan bahan sejauh 2 meter. Dalam hal ini, karena tidak diketahui waktu yang dibutuhkan secara langsung, maka kita dapat menggunakan rumus kecepatan sebagai berikut:

v = s/t

dengan v = kecepatan (m/s), s = jarak (m), dan t = waktu (s).

Kita sudah mengetahui nilai s yaitu 2 meter dan nilai v yaitu 1.39 kg/s. Selanjutnya kita tinggal mencari nilai t dengan menghitung berapa lama waktu yang dibutuhkan untuk memindahkan bahan sejauh 2 meter dengan kapasitas tersebut.

Kita dapat menggunakan rumus sebagai berikut:

kapasitas = massa / waktu

dengan kapasitas dalam satuan kg/s, massa dalam satuan kg, dan waktu dalam satuan s.

Dalam hal ini, karena kapasitas conveyor adalah 1.39 kg/s, maka massa bahan yang diangkut dalam satu detik adalah:

massa = kapasitas x waktu

1.39 kg/s = massa / waktu

waktu = massa / kapasitas

waktu = 150 kg / 1.39 kg/s

waktu ≈ 108 s

Setelah mengetahui nilai t yaitu sekitar 108 detik atau sekitar 1.8 menit, kita dapat menghitung nilai kecepatan conveyor sebagai berikut:

v = s/t

v = 2 m /108 s

v ≈ 0.019 m/s

Jawaban:

Jadi,kecepatan dalam memindahkan bahan tersebut adalah 0.019 m/s
