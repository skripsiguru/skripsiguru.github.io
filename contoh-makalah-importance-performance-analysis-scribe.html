8. 5 contoh soal importance of recycling beserta jawabannya​


Penjelasan:

1. Why is recycling important for the environment?

Answer: Recycling is important for the environment because it helps reduce pollution and conserve natural resources.

2. How does recycling help in conserving energy?

Answer: Recycling helps in conserving energy by reducing the need for extracting, refining, and processing raw materials. This means less energy is required for manufacturing new products.

3. What are the benefits of recycling for the economy?

Answer: Recycling can create job opportunities and stimulate economic growth. It also reduces the cost of waste management and the need for landfill space, which ultimately saves money.

4. How does recycling contribute to the reduction of greenhouse gas emissions?

Answer: Recycling reduces the need for extracting and producing raw materials, which often emit greenhouse gases during the manufacturing process. By recycling, we can decrease these emissions and mitigate climate change.

5. What role does recycling play in preserving natural resources?

Answer: Recycling helps in preserving natural resources by reducing the demand for new materials. It helps conserve resources like timber, water, and minerals, which in turn protects ecosystems and maintains biodiversity.

19. contoh kalimat order of importance


Order of Importance adalah ide atau pemikiran atau langkah-langkah yang disusun berdasarkan skala prioritasnya. Biasanya saat menuliskan sebuah Order of importance, kita dapat memulainya dari hal yang paling penting ke hal yang kurang penting, atau dari hal yang kurang penting ke hal yang paling penting. Dalam penulisan order of Importance, akan lebih mudah jika dilampirkan sebuah grafik atau gambar yang menjelaskan informasi pada order of importance tersebut.


Pembahasan

Contoh kalimat Order of Importance adalah:

1. The company has a clearly laid out hierarchy. (Perusahaan memiliki struktur jabatan yang jelas.)

2. All major decisions go through the president, who controls the entire operation, but most daily decisions go to the board. (Semua keputusan besar harus melalui presiden, yang mengendalikan seluruh operasional, tetapi sebagian besar keputusan harian diambilalih oleh anggota dewan.)

3. Beneath the board members are the regional managers, who oversee the branch managers, who run each local branch. (Di bawah anggota dewan terdapat manajer regional, yang mengawasi manajer cabang. Manajer cabang bertugas menjalankan setiap cabang lokal.)

29. Apa yang dimaksud dengan gap analysis? Contohnya seperti apa jika diterapkan di perusahaan?


 adalah perbandingan kinerja aktual dengan kinerja potensial atau yang diharapkan.Analisa gap dapat juga diartikan sebagai perbandingan kinerja aktual dengan kinerja potensial atau yang diharapkan.

Sebagai metoda, analisa gap digunakan sebagai alat evaluasi bisnis yang menitikberatkan pada kesenjangan kinerja perusahaan saat ini dengan kinerja yang sudah ditargetkan sebelumnya.

Analisa gap terdiri dari tiga komponen faktor utama yaitu: 1). daftar karakteristik (seperti atribut, kompetensi, tingkat kinerja) dari situasi sekarang (apa yang saat ini), 2). daftar apa yang diperlukan untuk mencapai tujuan masa depan (apa yang harus), dan 3). daftar kesenjangan apa yang ada dan perlu diisi.

Contoh Analytical Exposition Text – The Importance of Internet for Education

(Thesis)

The impact of internet in education has been far reaching and still developing. It has created instant access to a wide variety of research information to help students learn.

(Argument)

Nowadays, internet access has created the potential for students to learn new material easily. The time students have at class is limited, so the teacher sometimes cannot give all the explanation needed. In this case, internet can help students to get more explanation.

(Argument)

Internet has become now way of life. In the future, on students’ working life, they will have to use internet. So it is wise for the teacher to give assignment which related to it. For example, teacher can ask students to give the assignment via e-mail.

(Reiteration)

Internet has big impact on education. There are many positive things students and teacher can get from it.
