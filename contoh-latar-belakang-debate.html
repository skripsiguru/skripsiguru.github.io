The other school of thought argues that the central stupa is a mandala, or sacred diagram. The debate about the central stupa of Borobudur is a complex one, and there is no easy answer. Both schools of thought have valid arguments, and it is likely that the central stupa serves multiple purposes.

Debate is a process that involves formal discussion on a particular topic. In a debate, opposing arguments are put forward to argue for opposing viewpoints.
